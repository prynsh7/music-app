Introduction

SHUBHANGI JADHAV

Born in a Musical family of Hubballi(Karnataka), Shubhangi is one of the promising vocalists of Kirana Gharana. She was initiated into Music at a tender age of 8 by her grandfather, the Musical Maestro late Pt. D. R. Warang and her mother Smt. Sharada Jadhav. Influenced by the Kirana Gharana Tradition of her home town Dharwad , Shubhangi showed a great inclination towards the Kirana Gharana Gayaki and moved ahead to receive the talim for the same.

Choosing her career with Hindustani Classical Music, she stepped ahead and moved to Thane - Mumbai, in the year 2009 with an intention of learning Indian Classical Music. She was fortunate to receive the Talim of Kirana Gharana in authentic Guru-Shishya Parampara from Vidushi Smt. Vibhavari Bandhavkar , a Senior Disciple of Surmani Pt. Achyut K. Abhyankar (senior most disciple of Pt. Firoz Dastur). She stayed with her guru for 8 years, where She went through rigorous training and was groomed in Khyal Gaayaki. She also received her training in semi classical and light music forms. Shubhangi’s Intense dedication and quest to learn assured her guru about her bright musical future.

Shubhangi has enriched her musical dimensions and has emerged as a promising artist of the great Kirana Gharana tradition. With a clear, open and mellifluous voice of a wide range and depth, Shubhangi has tried to shape a style of her own. She has a good fluency and command over her classical singing where she brings out the beauty and structure of each raag with correct insights. She also contributes in Semi Classical singing forms such as Thumari, Jhula, Dadra etc with ease and melody. Shubhangi has established herself as a rising talent on the scene of Hindustani Classical Music and She is constantly trying to give her audience a captivating and soulful performance.
