About

SHUBHANGI ACADEMY OF MUSIC

Shubhangi Jadhav established her own Music Academy in the year 2016. Her purpose behind this academy is to teach and nurture Indian classical music in an authentic Guru Shishya Parampara. The academy aims to promote and spread Indian Classical Music in every possible way. It intends to propagate Hindustani classical music to the students and to encourage them to learn a variety of subjects related to Hindustani classical music, develop their personal performance style, and receive an excellent learning experience.

Music lessons are not just taught but are imbibed into the learners. The students are trained according to their capacity. Individual attention is the basic priority given. The academy is not just a format of class but a place where the art is taught and learnt with absolute devotion and dedication. The teaching process is more inclined towards developing each student as a good performer. The academy has various activities which include Riyaz session, voice culture, Ghar Baithaks, workshops etc for the overall development of the students.

The academy effectively provides both offline and online classes and is constantly striving to provide the best music education to all students. As a result, all the students are happy and satisfied with their learning here. The academy has been playing a major role in transforming the beginners to stage performers.
